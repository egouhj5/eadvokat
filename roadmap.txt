optimisations :

No Horizontal Scrolling: A user should never have to scroll left or right to see content on mobile.

Tap Targets: Buttons and links must be large enough (at least 48x48 pixels) and have enough space around them so users don't accidentally tap the wrong link.

Font Sizes: Text should never drop below 12px (ideally stay at 16px for body text on mobile) so users don't have to pinch-to-zoom.

Test with Google Search Console: Once your site is live, use Google Search Console's "URL Inspection" tool to verify that Google considers your pages mobile-friendly.
